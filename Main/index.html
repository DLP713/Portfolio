About Me

I am an energetic educator passionate about the accessibility of high quality education and the social impact of state-of-the-art technology in varied sociocultural contexts. I am a creative problem solver, trained anthropologist, and a fluent Spanish speaker interested in working towards a more equitable society while helping individuals reach their full potential.

I am passionate about amplifying the voice of marginalized stakeholders in matters that pertain to them. In education, I work towards cultivating policy and systems wide changes to ensure that students' educational outcomes are not dictated by their zip code. In web development, this means ensuring that all web design is accessible and culturally relevant to its users; minimizing barriers and increasing accessibility for all. I use my professional experience and anthropology education to use an interdisciplinary approach towards solving any problem I come across.
